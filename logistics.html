Logistics

Time

mBIT will be hosted on Saturday, June 8, 2024 from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM EDT. The competition will be simultaneously hosted in-person at Montgomery Blair High School and online on codeforces.com. The competition is open to teams of four and is offered at two divisions. Register now!

Competition

The competition will be 3 hours (non-consecutive) long and will consist of a number of programming challenges that teams must work together to solve. For specifics on grading, scoring, and prizes, read the competition rules and check out the problems in our archive.

Registration

Registration is now open. Create only one account per team. Once you have created the team account, choose a team name and fill out information on each member in the Profile section. Share the account username and password with your teammates. You may edit your team information at any time.

If you have any questions, please send us an email at mbit.organizers@gmail.com.


Organized by students at Montgomery Blair High School.
